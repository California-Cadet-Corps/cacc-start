# 🐛 Bug Report Template

> Found something broken? Awesome — reporting it helps the team! Copy this into a
> new [Issue](https://github.com/California-Cadet-Corps/cacc-start/issues/new/choose)
> or share it with your mentor.

---

## What's wrong? (one sentence)
(e.g. "The Join button doesn't do anything when I click it.")

## Where did it happen?
(e.g. "On the homepage, on my phone." Add the page link if you can.)

## Steps to make it happen again
1. Go to ________________
2. Click / type ________________
3. See the problem

## What I EXPECTED to happen
(e.g. "It should take me to the join page.")

## What ACTUALLY happened
(e.g. "Nothing happened — the page just stayed the same.")

## Extras that help (optional)
- 📸 Screenshot or screen recording (drag it into the issue)
- 💻 What device/browser? (e.g. "iPhone, Safari" or "Windows laptop, Chrome")
- ⚠️ Any error message you saw? (copy-paste the whole thing)

---

> 💡 The more detail you give, the faster someone can fix it. Great bug reports are a
> gift to the team! 🎁
