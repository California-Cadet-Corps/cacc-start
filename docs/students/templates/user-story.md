# 📖 User Story Template

> A user story describes a feature from the *user's* point of view. Copy the formula
> and fill in the three blanks.

---

## The formula

> **As a** [type of user],
> **I want** [to do something],
> **so that** [I get some benefit].

## Examples to copy the style from

- *As a* **cadet**, *I want* **to see a calendar of events**, *so that* **I don't miss a meeting.**
- *As a* **visitor**, *I want* **a big "Join" button**, *so that* **I can sign up quickly.**
- *As a* **parent**, *I want* **to find a contact email**, *so that* **I can ask questions.**
