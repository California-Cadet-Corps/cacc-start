About the Corps — California Cadet Corps

Founded in 1911, the California Cadet Corps is the oldest youth leadership organization in the state of California. Operating under the authority of the California Military Department, we have been developing the next generation of leaders for over a century.

Our cadets — drawn from high schools across California — learn discipline, teamwork, and civic responsibility through rigorous training programs modeled on U.S. Army and Air Force standards. Leadership, Excellence, and Service are not merely words; they are the foundation of everything we do.

Whether pursuing a military career or excelling in civilian life, graduates of the California Cadet Corps carry with them the values and skills forged during their time in the program. Join us and discover what you are capable of.
